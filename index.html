About us

Who We are

Rachel, founder of DanceDots, originally trained in Professional Musical Theatre at Doreen Bird College in London. Since working in various jobs across the industry, she saw an opportunity to develop an app which could aid performers in of all kinds of jobs.
Currently working in an events role, Rachel has brought her business knowledge and hopes to combine it with the arts to create a new tool for performers.

"I am still passionate about the arts even though I gave it up professionally. I enjoy singing and dancing recreationally but would love to build on my existing knowledge of the industry to provide solutions to out-dated processes."

Rachel
